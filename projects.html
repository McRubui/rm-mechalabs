Featured Project Case Study

Smart Conveyor Sorting & Automation System

Client Type: Small-Scale Manufacturing Facility

Industry: FMCG Production

Project Visual Overview

Problem

Manual product sorting caused bottlenecks, inconsistent classification, and high labor dependency during high production cycles.

Solution Delivered

  • Custom mechanical conveyor frame (CAD designed)
  • Sensor-based real-time detection system
  • PLC-controlled automated sorting gates
  • Modular scalability architecture

Before vs After Performance

Before Implementation
  • Manual sorting
  • High labor cost
  • Sorting inconsistencies
  • Production bottlenecks
After Implementation
  • Automated product classification
  • ↓ 40% labor dependency
  • ↑ 32% throughput increase
  • Consistent product flow

ROI & Business Impact

Estimated automation cost recovery achieved within 8–12 months due to labor reduction and throughput improvement.

  • Operational cost savings annually
  • Scalable infrastructure for expansion
  • Improved production reliability
  • Reduced human error margin
